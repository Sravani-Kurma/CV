Biography

SRAVANI KURMA (IEEE Member) is an Assistant Research Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Mississippi State University (MSU). She received the B.Tech. degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from JNTUH College of Engineering, Jagtial, India, in 2017, and the M.Tech. degree in Communication System Engineering from Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur, India, in 2021, where she was a Gold Medalist. She received the Ph.D. degree in Wireless Communications from the Institute of Communications Engineering, National Sun Yat-sen University, Taiwan, in 2024, where she received the Excellent Ph.D. Research Award. She worked as a Wi-Fi Firmware Engineering Intern at NXP Semiconductor, Taipei, Taiwan, from September 2023 to May 2024. Her current research interests include Agentic AI, 5G/6G wireless systems, AI-native and digital twin-enabled O-RAN, reconfigurable intelligent surfaces, cell-free massive MIMO, full-duplex communications, integrated sensing and communications, UAV-assisted intelligent networks, cyber-physical systems, vehicular communications, mobile edge computing, large language models, and machine learning for wireless communications.
